📘 Corsair Airlines Customer Service – Full Guidance & Contact Options

Need support from Corsair Airlines? ✈️ Whether you're in France, North America, or traveling internationally, this guide provides all the contact options to help you get the assistance you need. 👇

📞 Call Us

You can reach Corsair customer service via the following numbers:

  • 🇫🇷 From France: 3917 (charged at €0.35/min from landlines)
  • 🌍 International: +1-888-829-1117
  • 🇺🇸 North America: +1 888-657-8380
  • 🏢 Local Offices (New York, Los Angeles): +1 888-657-8380

📧 Email Support

If you prefer to write, you can email our team at:

corsair.res@apg-ga.ca

🔍 Please include the following details for faster service:

  • 👤 Full name
  • 🎫 Booking reference
  • 🛫 Flight information
  • 📝 Description of your issue

⏰ Operating Hours

  • 🇫🇷 France: Open daily from 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM (local time)
  • 🇺🇸 North America: Open Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM (local time)

🛬 Airport Customer Service

Need urgent help at the airport? Visit one of our service desks for support with:

  • 🔄 Flight changes or cancellations
  • 🛄 Baggage issues
  • ✅ Check-in problems

💡 Be sure to bring your booking reference and ID for faster assistance.
